Counselor Connected

Policies

Counselors must...

  • Keep information confidential unless legal requirements demand otherwise.
  • Report if the student intends to harm themselves or others.
  • Convey highly sensitive information through phone call or private conversation.
  • Not be related to the student immediately.
  • Provide a list of resources for outside agencies for students in distress.
  • Report incidents of bullying, dating violence, child abuse, and sexual harassment.
